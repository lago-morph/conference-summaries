Lightning Talk: Keycloak and EUDI-Wallet: A Match Made in Heaven? - Dominik Schlosser, Freelancer

The EU Digital Identity Wallet is coming and will soon allow EU citizens to authenticate to government services using a standardized and decentralized wallet infrastructure. Keycloak, as one of the most widely used IAM solutions, is expected to support this and has long begun to implement the necessary standards like OpenID 4 Verifiable Credentials Issuance (OpenID4VCI).

Last year, one of my public services customers asked me to use what is already there, enhance it and implement a first working wallet authentication prototype inside Keycloak.

This talk will showcase the results, problems and future work.

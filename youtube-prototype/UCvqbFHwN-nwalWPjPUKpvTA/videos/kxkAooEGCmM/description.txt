KeycloakCon | Sponsored Keynote: Modernizing Railway IT Architecture: Secure-by-Design with Keycloak - Bernhard Denner, Hitachi Rail

Mission-critical railway management systems are increasingly interconnected, making cybersecurity more essential than ever.
We, at Hitachi Rail, have therefore rebuilt our products using secure-by-design principles, with Keycloak serving as a central component of our architecture.

Keycloak has also supported us in modernizing the operation and maintenance of our IT systems, running these management systems in the customers data-centers.

Regulations such as NIS2 and the CRA confirm that we are well positioned for the challenges ahead.
 
This talk highlights some IEC 62443-4-2 requirements that often challenge traditional IT architectures, and demonstrates how Keycloak addresses them effectively.

Although Keycloak alone does not guarantee full compliance, it plays a key role in realizing resilient, modern secure-by-design architectures.
